Background Profile

Dr. Omaba is currently an Associate Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Mathematics, College of Science, University of Hafr Al Batin, Saudi Arabia. He holds a PhD in Mathematics from Loughborough University, Leicestershire, United Kingodm. His research interests include Stochastic Partial Differential Equations, Fractional Calculus, Integral Equations, Special Functions, Fractional Inequalities, Probability Theory, Measure Theory, Analysis. Recently, I have developed some interest in data analysis and exploration via SQL, Tablaeu, Power BI, Python. This page contains some of the projects undertaken in my quest to becoming a data analyst.
